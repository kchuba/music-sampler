A Look at Good Charlotte

Press Photo by Good Charlotte

Good Charlotte is a pop-punk band that came to fame in the late 1990s. Twins Joel and Benji Madden are the faces of the band, and bring many stories of growing up in a broken home in Maryland to their songs. The brothers write powerful, emotional lyrics about family, growing up and finding success in Los Angeles that can deeply resonate with any audience. Over Good Charlotte's six albums, the band has showcased a diverse sound and style of music, from head-banging pop classics to accoustic ballads.
